History & Background

India has a rich historical heritage marked by beautiful palaces, grand mansions, and ancient temples built by various dynasties. These structures reflect the country's royal lifestyle, religious beliefs, and artistic excellence. Many of them are now famous tourist attractions visited from all over the world


Culture & Lifestyle

India has a rich and diverse culture with many languages, religions, and traditions. People celebrate colourful festivals, follow traditional customs, and enjoy music, dance, and local food. Indian lifestyle reflects unity in diversity and strong family values.

Interesting Facts

Some interesting points about this destination:

  • India is the largest democracy in the world
  • The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World
  • India gave the world Yoga, Ayurveda, and the concept of Zero (0).
  • India has 22 official languages and hundreds of dialects
  • India's ISRO launched the Mars Mission (Mangalyaan) at very low cost
  • India is the 7th largest country in the world by area
  • India has the largest number of temples in the world
  • Indian food is famous worldwide for its spices and variety.

Overview of India

India is known for its unity in diversity, where people of different religions, languages, and cultures live together. Indians are generally known for their hospitality, respect for traditions, and strong family values.

Quick Facts

Location:
It is situated approximately 11 km from Jaipur, Rajasthan.
History:
Amer was the former capital before Jaipur.
Connectivity:

Season:

Top Places Image

The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble monument in Agra, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
The Ajanta and Ellora Caves are ancient rock-cut caves in Maharashtra, famous for their beautiful Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain art and sculptures.
India Gate is a historic war memorial in New Delhi, built to honor Indian soldiers who sacrificed their lives in World War I.
Hooghly River is a major distributary of the Ganga River that flows through West Bengal and passes by the city of Kolkata.
Shimla and Manali are popular hill stations in Himachal Pradesh, famous for their scenic mountains, cool climate, and tourist attractions.
This is the Kandariya Mahadeva Temple, a famous Hindu temple at Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
This is the Umngot River (Dawki River) in Meghalaya, famous for its crystal-clear green water and boat rides.
